How I contribute

I am passionate about the use of machine learning and artificial intelligence in physical sciences and engineering. Many phenomena in the world can be considered as a dynamical system. These systems inherently contain huge amounts of useful data, and it's up to us to correctly gather and analyze them to our advantage. Machine learning and data-driven methods provide us with a great platform to extract meaningful information from data,. In recent years, we've witnessed an appeal in large industries to utilize these techniques in their line of work.

Above paragraph defines the main theme of my research work. By developing different intelligent models by the means of machine learning, I try to contribute to the state-of-the-art data-driven algorithms, and also to learn and acquire skills and knowledge necessary to make a difference in the world.
